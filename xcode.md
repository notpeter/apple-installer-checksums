# Xcode Installer Checksums

## What is this?

SHA1 and SHA256 checksums of installers for:

 * [macOS' InstallESD.dmg](readme.md)
 * [Xcode's Installer DMGs](#xcode-installer-checksums)

## Why?

These checksums are used to verify whether or not your copies of Xcode have been tampered with.

## Verify checksums

macOS:

`shasum Xcode*.xip`

Linux, etc:

`sha1sum Xcode*.xip`

Windows 10 (PowerShell 3+)

`Get-FileHash -Algorithm SHA1 Xcode*.xip`
